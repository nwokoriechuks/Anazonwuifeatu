Mission

We enhance customer service with AI-driven call analysis, using NVIDIA’s CUDA and Riva for real-time speech processing and Claude API for intelligent response suggestions—reducing resolution time by 50%.

Tech Stack

  • CUDA: Accelerates audio preprocessing (3x faster than CPU).
  • NVIDIA Riva: Enables <200ms speech-to-text conversion.
  • Claude API: Generates context-aware agent responses.

Use Cases

  • Call Centers: Auto-generates CRM notes and detects customer sentiment.
  • E-Commerce: Identifies frustration triggers in real time.

Performance

  • 200ms latency
  • 90% suggestion accuracy
  • 40% shorter calls

Why NVIDIA?

Our solution leverages CUDA, Riva, and Triton Inference Server, demonstrating GPU-accelerated AI for enterprise customer support.
